<p>Obviously, in a grouping sentence we can incorporate restrictions, with the clause <code>WHERE</code>. In this way, not all rows of the table will be used when calculating partial results, but only those that meet established restrictions.</p><h3>Example:</h3><p>In the example we used in the previous year, those employees who had no associates had any department.
