<p>When we establish the attribute by which we will order the results, we can indicate that the order is ascending (<code>ASC</code> or nothing) or descendant (<code>DESC</code>)</p><blockquote><p><code>SELECT <em>atributos</em><br/>FROM <em>tabla</em><br/>ORDER BY <em>atributo </em>[DESC | ASC ];</code></p></blockquote><h3>Example:</h3><p>We can verify that adding ASC to the previous sentence that ordered the regions by their REGION_NAME, does not vary the result.</p><blockquote><p><code>SELECT * FROM<br/>regions<br/>ORDER BY REGION_NAME ASC;</code></p></blockquote><table align="center" border="1px solid" summary="Results from query"><tbody><tr><th>REGION_ID</th><th>REGION_NAME</th></tr><tr><td>2</td><td>Americas</td></tr><tr><td>3</td><td>Asia</td></tr><tr><td>1</td><td>Europe</td></tr><tr><td>4</td><td>Middle East and Africa</td></tr></tbody></table><p>On the other hand, by adding DESC we managed to reverse the order showing the result:</p><blockquote><p><code>SELECT * FROM<br/>regions<br/>ORDER BY REGION_NAME DESC;</code></p></blockquote><table align="center" border="1px solid" summary="Results from query"><tbody><tr><th>REGION_ID</th><th>REGION_NAME</th></tr><tr><td>4</td><td>Middle East and Africa</td></tr><tr><td>1</td><td>Europe</td></tr><tr><td>3</td><td>Asia</td></tr><tr><td>2</td><td>Americas</td></tr></tbody></table><h3>Exercise:</h3><p>Displays the name (DEPARTMENT_NAME) of the departments (departments) sorted <strong>descending </strong>by the name.</p>
