<p>Functions <code>ROUND</code> and <code>TRUNC</code> are similar to those <code>FLOOR</code> and <code>CEIL</code>which we have used in the previous year, but they work with the possibility to indicate the number of decimals that we want to have the result:</p><ul><li><code>ROUND(n [,m])</code>: Returns the number <code>N</code> rounded to ‘m’ decimals.</li><li><code>TRUNC(n, [m])</code><code>: </code>Trunca number <code>N</code> to have a certain number of digits (with m decimals).</li></ul><h3>Example:</h3><p>Let's imagine the salary that appears in the attribute <code>SALARY</code> of the board of employees corresponds to the annual salary of 12 pays.
