Recordemos que, normalmente, la expresi&oacute;n hace referencia a un atributo.</p><h3>Ejemplos:</h3><p>&iquest;Cu&aacute;l es el salario m&aacute;ximo y m&iacute;nimo de los empleados del esquema <code>HR</code>?</p><blockquote><p><code>SELECT MAX(SALARY) AS &quot;MAX SALARY&quot;, MIN(SALARY) AS &quot;MIN SALARY&quot;<br />FROM employees;</code></p></blockquote><table align="center" border="1" summary="Results from query"><tbody><tr><th style="background-color:#9e9e9e;
