<p>Functions <code>FLOOR</code> and <code>CEIL</code> are very similar:</p><ul><li><code>FLOOR(n)</code>: Returns the entire value immediately below or equal to <code>n</code>.</li><li><code>CEIL(n)</code>: Returns the entire value immediately superior or equal to <code>n</code>.</li></ul><h3>Example:</h3><p>Let's imagine the salary that appears in the attribute <code>SALARY</code> of the board of employees corresponds to the annual salary of 12 pays.
